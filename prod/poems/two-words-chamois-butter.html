Two words: chamois butter

Mar 10, 2022

Hours spent in a saddle seated
Stomping, spinning, grinding, going
The jeans betray my rump, mistreat it
Need to prevent blisters from growing
A means to ease my frictioned scoot
For me, this calls for something new:
The superhero spandex suits.
Stretchy, sporty, comfy, dorky
I'd thought it was a sham
A ploy for cash, flex threads to chic your hams
I thought I'd try it and embolden me as rebutter
But my butt feels way better now, especially with the butter.
