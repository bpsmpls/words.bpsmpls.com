My chest is like a chair

Aug 01, 2014

My chest is like a chair. I'm withered from years of pressure.
Careless guests I entertain loosen the legs of my ribs, kicking out the meticulous alignment. The wonderful craftsmanship from old hands, rough in strength but paternal in touch.
To purging owners, I'm worn open all over and discarded streetside for a cold wait, possibly for another enthusiastic secondhand handyman to restore me to new glory again.
