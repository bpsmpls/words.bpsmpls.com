Quarantined

Jun 10, 2015

Here I am quarantined
Folded on the couch with only a digital reach
Whatever wakefulness I can muster between dizzy fever pass-outs is spent looking at a screen
Television, work laptop, handheld video games, cell phone
Bored as hell
Not awake long enough to read a book
Hell, I can barely read a 100 word article without dosing off
Outside it's a little grey, but warm with mild humidity
A teasing invite
Hey go ride your bike!
Oh right you're sick and your shoulder is busted.
Hey go meet up with some friends!
Oh right you're highly contagious.
Hey go lay down in your bed!
Oh right your bones hurt, willingly falling asleep isn't an option
