Receiving compliments

Dec 18, 2020

You hand me this orb,
A gift for which you ask no reciprocation.
The weight of it settles into my palms with urgency
Slipping only the slightest bit.
My fingertips grip its glassy surface.
Its curious glow blushes my hands.
A tingle trickles up my forearm and I stiffen.
It settles into a calm warmth.

What do I do with it?
I can't put it down anywhere or it will roll
Away or worse, attract damage.
I want to protect it,
Appreciate it,
Learn from it,
But it's getting heavy.
