Mind erasers

Apr 11, 2016

She called them mind erasers
At that Thai place nearby
They were two for one
When in Rome, so to speak
I remember the skip over here
But time skips after that
There's a picture of us at the base
Glass-eyed grinning wiley buffoons
Then at the top
We're wasted on the Space Needle
And I keep pretending I'll push you off
And you can't tell if I'm kidding
I can't hold the telescope straight
To see the Ferris wheel
I tripped over a kid
Don't eat the food
I feel like it was bad
But I don't remember
I had my mind erased
