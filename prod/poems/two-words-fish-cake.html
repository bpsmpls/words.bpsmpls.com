Two words: fish cake

Mar 10, 2022

I'm never taken seriously.
I have but just one wish.
An achievement for a trout:
To produce a magnificent cake.

You'll assume I speak deliriously,
But just because I'm a fish
Doesn't mean I know nothing about
The intricacies of how to bake.

My skill came quite mysteriously
I'd dropped a dirty dish
And scrubbing up the dirty grout
My instincts sprung awake.

My friends chide me furiously
They're somehow super pissed.
They clamor on, bang, and shout
That our tank will probably break.

They'll see just how luxuriously
How decadent and delish
They'll backtrack on their doubt
And beg me if they can partake.

...Er, the result came out... curiously.
The shape seems pretty squished.
And cooking pressure blew the glass out.
I may've made a huge mistake.
