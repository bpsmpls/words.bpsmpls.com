Two words: jurassic nebula

Mar 10, 2022

The monitor chirps.

¥«STEG-45, what is your alpha?»

I'm floating far beyond where I ever thought possible.
An unbelievable honor for me to go on this mission.
The study of the stars, up close.
Far from home.

¥«STEG, do you read?»

Sigh. But not quite far enough. 

&«Yeah, REX-7. I hear you. What do you need?»

¥«We need your coordinates.»

The monitor crackles behind me as I look out the port. It's beautiful.

¥«We can't see you on our charts. Interference maybe.»

All this way to look at one messy cloud. And this is the pinnacle of reptilian science. 
But it's neat all the same–
Wait.

&«REX...»

What is that?

&«There's something hurtling through the nebula.»

¥«Are you in danger, 45?»

Doing some quick math...
Shit.

&«Negative. But if my assessment is correct, you birds might have some serious worries back at home.»

It's coming fast. A couple years tops until it gets there.

&«There's a comet of incredible mass coming your way.»
