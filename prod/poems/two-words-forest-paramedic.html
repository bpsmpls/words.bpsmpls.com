Two words: forest paramedic

Jun 10, 2024

A tree tumbles amongst its friends.
It strikes the ground with furious weight.
The echo of its landing is sharp and quickly decays.
It feebly splays.

Far off on the other end
The red and blue awake.
Sprung to action, tense with haste fearing any delay,
"We're on the way."

In time, they arrive on scene to tend
But the roots could not endure the wait.
Its leaves are gone, dropped and blown far away.
Its bark has flayed.

Red climbs down from atop his friend.
And considers their luck today
Scratching his beard he says, "Babe,
Let's haul these logs away."
