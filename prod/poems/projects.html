Projects

Feb 18, 2017

Okay I have the plan.
I've sketched it out a dozen different times.
It's going to be perfect.
So usable.
So functional.
So flexible.

Okay I have the materials.
Loading stuff into the space.
Constructing, adjusting, arranging, rearranging, fine tuning...
Snack break.
These long late hours are paying off.
Step back and take it in.
Almost done.

Okay it's all where I want it.
It took some time,
But it's finally perfect.
Flexible.
Functional.
Usable.
Ideal.
But now what?
