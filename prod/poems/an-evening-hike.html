An evening hike

Apr 07, 2021

Evening hike after an afternoon bike
My sandals grip along webs of roots
And I intuit each's attributes
Steps up steep stone slabs
Other folks on foot or bike stop for chats
I disassemble decorative cairns
The summit's quick, the sunset shares
Quietness grows, my shoulders soften
I think, "why don't I do this more often?"
Without the roots, elevation, or rocks
Hikes at home feel like just a regular walk.
