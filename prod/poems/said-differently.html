Said differently

Apr 11, 2016

That's not how you say it
Say it differently
How do I say it?
Say it like that
Your way isn't right
It doesn't fit
I don't know what it is
But it's wrong
I don't have time to check it over
It's complex in its wrongness
All I know is you can't say it like that
They told me that
So I'm telling you
Stop before you embarrass yourself
Say it differently
Try it again
That's not how you say it.
Try it again
Forget it.
