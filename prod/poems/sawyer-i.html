Sawyer I

May 06, 2016

On the docks at the front of the lake
Slipping your feet into the black waves
Look out for the ducks, even if they're just checking in
The cloppy plunger flutter of the water hitting the dock
Far away from us answering what we look for in a person

Above and below

Musings of Manarchy

The book that will chronicle this day
Will be called Dinosaurs and Food Metaphors
